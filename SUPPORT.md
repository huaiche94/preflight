# Support

> 🌐 English | [繁體中文](SUPPORT.zh-TW.md)

Auspex is an early-stage open-source project. Support is
community-based and best-effort.

## Where to ask

- **Bugs and feature requests** — open a
  [GitHub issue](https://github.com/huaiche94/auspex/issues). Include
  `auspex version` output, your OS, and (for runtime problems) the
  output of `auspex doctor --json` with anything sensitive removed.
- **Questions about architecture or contributing** — read
  [`README.md`](README.md), [`CONSTITUTION.md`](CONSTITUTION.md), and
  [`docs/design/Auspex_ADD.md`](docs/design/Auspex_ADD.md) first; then open an issue if the
  answer isn't there.
- **Security vulnerabilities** — do **not** open a public issue. Follow
  [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).

## What to expect

- Issues are triaged as maintainer time allows; there is no SLA.
- Auspex stores all state locally (SQLite under your OS user-data
  directory). Support bundles and diagnostics never include raw prompts
  by design — please keep it that way when pasting logs into issues.
